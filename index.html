EARTH

Earth, our home in the vastness of the cosmos, is the third planet from the Sun in our solar system. A unique celestial body, it is the only known world that harbors life. With a diverse tapestry of ecosystems, from oceans teeming with marine life to continents adorned with lush forests and expansive deserts, Earth is a haven of natural wonders.
